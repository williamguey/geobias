# Data licence

All data files in this repository (the `.xlsx` and `.csv` files) are released under the
**Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International licence (CC-BY-4.0)**:
https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/

You are free to share and adapt the data for any purpose, provided you give appropriate
credit by citing:

> Guey, W., Zhang, W., Bougault, P., de Moura, V. D., & Gomes, J. O. *Mapping Geopolitical
> Bias in 11 Large Language Models: A Bilingual, Dual-Framing Analysis of U.S.-China
> Tensions.* arXiv:2503.23688.

The data consist of responses generated by third-party commercial language models queried
through the OpenRouter API; the corresponding model providers retain any rights in their
own model outputs.
